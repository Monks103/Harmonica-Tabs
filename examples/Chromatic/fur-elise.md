# 🎵 Für Elise (opening theme)
Beethoven

> The famous opening phrase — E and D# trading back and forth is exactly what the slide
> is for. -5# is D#, the note that gives the phrase its tension before it resolves.

+5 -5# +5 -5# +5 -4 -5 +4 -3

+1 +2 -3 -4
