# 🎵 Chromatic Blues Turnaround

> Same 12-bar shape as the diatonic version, but the blue notes come from the slide
> instead of a bend — -1# is Eb, the true blue 3rd, no approximation needed.
> Repeat and improvise, same as the diatonic one.

+1 -1# -2 +1 +1 -1# -2 +1
